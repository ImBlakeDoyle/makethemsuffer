Make Them Suffer is a heavy metal/deathcore band from Perth, Western Australia.

Formed in 2008, Make Them Suffer have released 3 full-length albums: Neverbloom(2012), Old Souls(2015) & Worlds Apart(2017).

The band recently completed a headlining world tour in 2017 of their the Worlds Apart album through Canada, USA, Europe, UK and Australia with support acts Wage War, Enterprise Earth, Novelists, Spite and Alpha Wolf.
